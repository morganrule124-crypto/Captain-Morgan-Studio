About the Artist

Morgan Rule is an acrylic landscape artist inspired by the natural beauty of the Georgia coast. Through large-scale, colorful canvases, Morgan captures the quiet atmosphere of marshes, skies, waterways, and ocean horizons using a soft sunrise palette.

Morgan is an OUPV captain from the Georgia Lowcountry and is currently majoring in marine biology with a focus on scientific diving. Time spent navigating coastal waters and studying marine systems informs the work—shaping both subject matter and an attention to light, movement, and place.
